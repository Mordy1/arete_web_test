Frequently Asked Questions

General questions

How to Incorporate a New Company?

Applicant will have 2 options
1. Direct incorporation – where applicant directly apply for incorporation to SSM
2. Name reservation – where applicant reserve a proposed name with SSM before incorporation.
The process will be hassle free by using our platform. Just sign-up, get started and we will take care the rest.

What are the Key Requirements to Incorporate a New Private Limited Company (Sdn Bhd)?

- 1 or more directors, must at least 18yo (Min of director number must reside in Malaysia)
- 1-50 shareholders (can be local, foreigner & corporate body)
- Minimum paid-up capital is RM1.00
- A registered address in Malaysia

Who qualifies to be a director?

To be a director of a private limited company in Malaysia, you must be at least 18yo, reside in Malaysia, are not an undischarged bankrupt, have not been disqualified by the Court or convicted of an offense involving bribery, fraud, or dishonesty.

How long to incorporate a Private Limited Company?

After submitting all related information to SSM, usually it takes 1-3 working days provided there is no query from SSM and subject to SSM officer and approval.

What name can I use for my company?

You may refer to the guideline from SSM before naming your company

Can a foreigner set-up a company in Malaysia?

Yes, the individual must be above 18yo and the individual named as the director of company should have a principal or only place of residence within Malaysia.

Can I incorporate a company with 1 Director and 1 Shareholder?

Yes, provided that the sole director must reside in Malaysia

What should I do after my company is incorporated?

- Within 30days after incorporation date, appoint your first company secretary
- Lodgement of constitution (where applicable) RM200
- Open bank account- Register with LHDN, KWSP, Custom etc (where applicable)
